Durga puja begins from the sixth day which is “Shashti” and ends on the tenth day
            which is “Vijayadashami”. It teaches people to fight with evil and come forward
            with the real victory in life. People begin this festival by installing many handiwork
            goddess Durga idols in various pandals.
